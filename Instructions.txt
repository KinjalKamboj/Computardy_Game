Welcome to Computardy! This game is a single-player review game 
designed to allow you to review important concepts in Grade 12 
Computer Science. To get started, please go back and click on the 
start buzzer button. You will be directed to the main game where 
there are 12 questions, three in each category, ranging from 
easy (150), medium (250) and hard (350). Select a question 
and choose the best answer from each multiple choice. You have 
45 seconds to answer each question. It would be ideal to achieve
a minimum score of 2050 points. You can choose to exit the 
game anytime by clicking the exit button or closing the frame. 
		   Good luck!
